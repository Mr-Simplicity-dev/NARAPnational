The Nigerian Association of Refrigeration and Air conditioning Practitioners (NARAP)

NARAP, The Nigerian Association of Refrigeration and Air conditioning Practitioners (NARAP) is a non-political, non-sectarian and non-governmental Organization. It was incorporated on 20th October 1993, as an umbrella Association for all categories of practitioners, Engineers, Technologists, Technicians, craft men and women and other persons producing and trading in, or practicing Refrigeration and Air-conditioning in Nigeria, encompassing all areas of profession.

Administrative Structure

The Association has its National structures with Headquarters located in Lagos, and offices in the six Geo-Political zones. It also has its State Chapters, Zones in all the Local Government Areas, and Ward throughout the federation. Membership at the Ward level is mandatory for all practitioners, individuals and corporate bodies engaged in formidable management structure for effective and efficient dissemination of information and easy administration, from the Board of Trustees, National, State Chapters, Zonal Councils and Ward Executive Committee. The National president and his Executive officers are the overall overseers of day to day running of the Association, (see the Association Organogram).

Our mission & Vision

At The NARAP, we establish, develop, promote and regulate Professional ethics of Refrigeration and Air-conditioning Production, Trades and Practices in Nigeria.

We create awareness of Academic, Commercial and Professional responsibilities, and generate the spirit of national development amongst members. To rank with other Refrigeration and Air-conditioning bodies in the world. To create awareness of Technological breakthrough in Refrigeration & Air-Conditioning Practices. To enhance professional responsibilities and adhere to international best practices. To foster the spirit of comradeship for National development.
